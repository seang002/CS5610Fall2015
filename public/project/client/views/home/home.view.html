About

We walk. We sit. We can even stay!

Happy Paws is a new upcoming dog-walking service for the greater Seattle area in Dec 2015! Everyone knows a dog is a man's best friend. We love them, we care for them... they're basically like our family. But of course, sometimes work or play gets in the way and the dog is left behind. But that's where we step in! Happy Paws is dedicated to making it quick and easy for you and your pup to find a dog walker in your neighborhood that you can book once, daily, or weekly! Sign up now and get started!

The humans.

Our Happy Paws team take our job very seriously. Each member is a long-time pet-owner themselves or has been exposed to petcare for more than 5 years. We believe that the friendship between a human and a dog is special and will create an individualized bond with each dog. All of our walkers have been background-checked, so you can rest at ease and be confident in our dog-walking service!
